Yia Yia's Tzatziki Sauce

Description

Straight from Greece! This recipe has been in my family for years. My Auntie showed me how to make it this Greek Easter. Great with raw red peppers and pita chips.

Nutrition Facts: Per Serving: 107 calories; protein 2.3g; carbohydrates 2.5g; fat 10g; cholesterol 20.1mg; sodium 31.2mg.

Steps

Step 1: Peel cucumbers and grate into a colander. Squeeze out excess water.

Step 2: Mix together sour cream, yogurt, garlic, and olive oil in a large bowl. Stir in cucumbers. Chill at least 30 minutes and up to 2 hours before serving.

Ingredients

  • 2 English cucumbers
  • 2 (16 ounce) containers sour cream
  • 1 (16 ounce) container Greek yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons minced garlic
  • 1 tablespoon extra virgin olive oil
